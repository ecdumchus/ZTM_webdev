Some of My Favorite Creatures

Rescue organizations have many wonderful pets waiting for a forever home. These are some organizations I have experience with and highly recommend:
Hope for the Animals
Animal Alliance
Southern Comfort Rescue

Max was our first rescue. He lived to be 17 1/2.
We found Max through Hope for the Animals.

Sammy was our second rescue.
Sadly, we lost him to a very aggressive form of lymphoma when he was only 6.
Sammy came to us through Animal Alliance.

Alfie is our current rescue.
He is such a happy little guy, he makes everyone he meets smile,
and tell him he is absolutely adorable. Alfie just celebrated his first birthday.
We found Alfie through Southern Comfort Rescue.
